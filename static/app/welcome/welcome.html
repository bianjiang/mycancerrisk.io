How much do you know about your Colorectal Cancer risk?

Welcome! Thank you for your interest in the “Developing a Facebook app to promote colorectal cancer screening: pilot testing a social media intervention” study.

The purpose of this study is to help researchers understand what people know about colorectal cancer so that they can develop materials to prevent and detect the disease. If you agree to be in this study, you would be asked to participate in a pre-study telephone interview, use a Facebook app, and a post-study telephone interview. Both telephone interviews should only take 15-20 minutes each. The Facebook app sends 1 Facebook message per month over 6 months. Participants will receive a $10.00 gift card for each telephone survey completed. There will be two surveys for a total of $20.00. There is no cost to you to participate in this research study.
